TEEPTRAK PARTNER AGREEMENT

1. PARTNERSHIP SCOPE

This Partner Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between TeepTrak SAS ("TeepTrak") and the Partner identified in the Partner Portal registration. This Agreement governs the terms under which Partner may refer, promote, and sell TeepTrak products and services.

2. PARTNER OBLIGATIONS

Partner agrees to: (a) complete all required training and certification programs; (b) maintain professional standards in all customer interactions; (c) accurately represent TeepTrak products and capabilities; (d) comply with all applicable laws and regulations; (e) protect TeepTrak confidential information.

3. DEAL REGISTRATION & PROTECTION

Partners may register opportunities through the Partner Portal. Approved registrations receive 90-day deal protection. During this period, the registered Partner is the exclusive partner for that opportunity. Protection may be extended upon request with valid justification.

4. COMMISSION STRUCTURE

Commission rates are determined by Partner tier: Bronze (15%), Silver (20%), Gold (25%), Platinum (30%). Commissions are calculated on net contract value and paid within 30 days of customer payment receipt.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Partner is granted a limited, non-exclusive license to use TeepTrak trademarks and marketing materials solely for partnership activities. All intellectual property rights remain with TeepTrak.

6. TERM & TERMINATION

This Agreement has an initial term of one (1) year and automatically renews for successive one-year periods unless terminated by either party with 30 days written notice. Either party may terminate immediately for material breach.

7. CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of proprietary information shared during the partnership. This obligation survives termination of the Agreement.

Electronic Signature

By signing, you agree to be bound by the terms of this agreement. A copy will be sent to your registered email address.
